What is Myint Mo?

Myint Mo is an online refer investment. You can get pay or earn by refer friends or people you know.

Minimum recharge

There have minimum recharge for any new user, the minimum recharge is 3000Ks.

Level in Myint Mo

There have 3 level in Myint Mo.

  • Level 1 (50% per refer, without commission)
  • Level 2 (50% per refer, 16.67% commission)
  • Level 3 (50% per refer, 33.36% commission)

At level 1, you will get 1500Ks if you refer a person to become your member, you won't get any commission from your member!

At level 2, you will get 1500Ks if you refer a person to become your member, you will get 500Ks(16.67%) commission from your members.

At level 3, you will get 1500Ks if you refer a person to become your member, you will get 1000Ks(33.36%) commission from your members.

Maximum requirements for upgrad level

You must have at last 3 members to upgrade level from 1 to 2!

You must have at last 10 members to upgrade level from 2 to 3!

Minimum for Withdraw

We doesn't limit any amount of withdraw, so you can withdraw any amount of balance that you want.
